    I am Richard Kanai, a self-taught front-end web developer skilled in the latest skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript. I started learning coding in 2017 while taking my computer science course at Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology, Kenya and fell in love with it.     I combine my problem-solving skills, engineering and programming skills in all my work.

    I enjoy creating websites the help a user achieve specified goals conveniently. I also love integrating APIs into websites for data usage and management. I tutor students who are getting started with web development. I use Firebase for most backend needs of my projects and Netlify for hosting.

It shows the some information about a country, cases, deaths, fully and partially vaccinated people and shows percentage of the vaccinated population in a country.
    Note: Searching for a country outside Africa gives results too but the table in the site shows numbers for African Countries only.

    Food Recipe Search Site
